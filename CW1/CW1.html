Guardians Of The Galaxy

Following the "Star Lord" himself, We see Peter Quill (Chris Pratt) forming an alliance with like minded criminals in a quest to find an Orb. Once they learn about the great power of this orb, it becomes their mission to save the universe from mass destruction. Meet the group of unlinkely superheros:

  • Rocket Racoon - Gun Excentric Extraordinaire
  • Gamora - Daughter of Thanos
  • Drax The Destroyer - Revenge Seeking Creature
  • and everyones favourite: Groot
